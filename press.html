In the press

Single-handedly, Fishlove has managed to do what nothing else has: to put the issue of fish and marine conservation on the front covers of newspapers and magazines across the globe.

 
  • Huffington Post US
  • Perez Hilton US
  • The Guardian UK
  • Yahoo Australia Australia
  • El Nacional Venezuela
  • Evening Standard UK
  • The Sunday Times Magazine UK
  • The Sunday Times UK
  • Daily Star UK
  • El Intransigente Argentina
  • El Sabado Portugal
  • Ocean2012 European Union
  • Photo France

“I wanted to congratulate you on the massive amount of coverage you are getting for the latest round of images – amazing! As I said to you on the phone when we spoke in the summer I think it is a tremendous way to get members of the public to think about the issues, and start connecting with the issue of destructive fishing practices! Now on to Russia and China where some of the major sources of destructive fishing lie – particularly the latter.”

Kristian Teleki Director of Global Engagement, Global Ocean Commission
